About

System thinking
meets execution.

Over 20 years designing and building digital products — from early-stage concepts through scalable design systems. The through-line has always been the same: reduce friction between strategy and what ships.

For 13 years I ran teken, a digital product studio operating across SaaS, B2B, agribusiness and logistics. I built and maintained design systems, defined product vision across client contexts, and made product decisions under real resource constraints.

Today my work sits at the intersection of product architecture, interface design, and AI-augmented workflows. I believe the designer who knows how to name what they see doesn't depend solely on the screen to build.
