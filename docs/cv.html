Profile

I work at the architecture layer — where UX, operations and AI need to connect into something that holds under growth. Not redesigning screens. Restructuring how the product thinks.

When I enter a project, something is usually breaking: teams deciding in silos, flows built on patches, AI added as a feature instead of embedded into the logic. I map where structure is failing and rebuild it so the product scales without accumulating complexity.
