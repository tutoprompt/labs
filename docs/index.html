About

System thinking
meets execution.

Over 20 years designing and building digital products, from early-stage concepts to scalable design systems. The through-line has always been the same: reduce friction between strategy and what ships.

For 13 years I ran teken, a digital product studio operating across SaaS, B2B, agribusiness and logistics. I built and maintained design systems, defined product vision across multiple client contexts, and made product decisions under real resource constraints.

Today my work sits at the intersection of product architecture, interface design and AI-augmented workflows. The designer who knows how to name what they see doesn't depend solely on the screen to build.

Process note

I set Figma aside as the starting point. Not because it is bad, but to test how far I could go without it. The result: think, prompt, browser. Fewer intermediaries, more proximity to the final result.
How I Work

AI is not a shortcut in this process. It is infrastructure. Each model has a specific cognitive role. Each tool sits exactly where it reduces friction.

Thinking
ChatGPT Exploring ideas, mapping problems, generating initial frameworks
Claude Structured reasoning, alternative perspectives, contrasting conclusions
Research
Perplexity Market signals, competitive context and domain references with sources
Gemini Cross-referencing findings, expanding scope when sources fall short
Manus Structured survey flows and stakeholder input gathering
Building
Claude Interface architecture, logic structure, HTML/CSS/JS outputs
Lovable Rapid scaffolding from concept to testable output in one session
GitHub Copilot Inline completion and refactoring inside the editor
Figma Make Component logic and design-to-code translation from Figma
Visual Creation
Figma Design system architecture, component libraries, production specs
Framer Interactive prototypes and motion-rich design presentations
Sora Visual and motion asset generation at a scale no traditional team could match
Planning
Manus Turning blurry ideas into structured flows and navigable low-fi prototypes
Claude Documentation, decision logs and structural summaries as systems grow
ChatGPT Roadmap brainstorming, prioritization frameworks, strategic framing

Prompt Design · Ongoing experiment

Building a vocabulary of prompts for design. Typography, spacing, hierarchy, UX intent. Visual decisions turned into language, ready to iterate directly in the browser.

Models are assigned roles based on where they reason best. The goal is structural clarity, not model loyalty. A working library of these decisions, open and evolving: github.com/royvillasana/figma-to-ai-prompter
